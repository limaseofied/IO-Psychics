What is a Guide?

Our Guides are skilled psychics mediums online who possess the profound ability to illuminate life's paths with compassion and insight. They foster connections that transcend the ordinary, guiding our Seekers (individuals seeking psychic guidance) toward profound understanding and empowerment.

Psychic online reading sessions offer just as much insight as in-person readings.

When you connect with our experienced psychic readers online, you'll receive guidance from the comfort of your own home at a time that works best for you and without the need to travel.

How can a psychic reading help me?

A psychic reader's online session can be a powerful tool for gaining insight into various aspects of your life.

  • Clarity and perspective: A psychic reading can provide you with clarity and a fresh perspective on your situation, helping you see things from a new angle.
  • Validation and confirmation: Receive confirmation of your intuition and inner knowing, validating your feelings and providing reassurance that you're on the right path.
  • Empowerment and self-discovery: Gain insights into your strengths, weaknesses, and untapped potential so you can make confident decisions and live authentically.
  • Healing and closure: Whether you're struggling with past traumas, unresolved emotions, or lingering questions about a loved one who has passed, a psychic reading can offer peace.
  • Manifestation and goal setting: Identify your goals, uncover any blocks or obstacles standing in your way, and develop a plan of action to achieve success.
What types of psychic readings are available?

We offer a wide range of psychic readings tailored to meet the diverse needs of our Seekers, including:

  • Tarot card readings: Tarot cards are a useful tool for gaining insight into your past, present, and future. Our experienced Tarot readers interpret the cards' symbolism to provide guidance and clarity on various aspects of your life.
  • Clairvoyant/clairaudient/clairsentient readings: Our clairvoyant, clairaudient, and clairsentient psychics possess heightened intuitive abilities to see, hear, and feel information beyond the physical realm.   • Mediumship: Mediumship readings connect you with the spirit world, allowing you to communicate with departed loved ones, spirit guides, and angels. Our mediums serve as conduits for messages of love, healing, and guidance from the other side.
  • Empath readings: Empaths possess a deep understanding of emotions and energy, allowing them to tune into your feelings and experiences. Our empath Guides provide compassionate support and insight to help you navigate through emotional challenges and find inner peace.
  • Dream exploration: Dreams are windows to the subconscious mind, offering valuable insights and messages from your higher self. Our dream exploration readings help you decode the symbolism of your dreams and uncover hidden truths about yourself and your life.
  • Astrology and numerology: Astrology and numerology readings provide insights into your personality, life path, and destiny based on the positions of celestial bodies and numerical patterns.
  • Crystals, pendulums, oracles, and runes: These divination tools are used to channel spiritual energy and provide guidance on specific questions or concerns. Our Guides expertly interpret the messages conveyed through these tools to offer clarity and direction.
  • Reiki and aura cleansing: Reiki sessions and aura cleansing sessions promote healing and balance by clearing negative energy and restoring harmony to your mind, body, and spirit.
  • Remote viewing: Remote viewing readings allow our Guides to perceive information about distant or unseen locations, events, and objects using extrasensory perception. Whether you're seeking answers about a lost item or insights into a future event, these sessions can provide valuable insights.

What can I expect during an online psychic reading?

When you meet with psychic mediums online, you can expect a personalized and insightful experience that offers clarity, guidance, and support.

The session typically begins with a warm greeting and an introduction to your Guide. They will encourage you to share any areas of your life where you're seeking guidance. They may ask you questions to better understand your situation and concerns.

Using their psychic abilities, your Guide will tune into your energy and provide intuitive insights and guidance tailored to your specific needs and circumstances. Throughout the reading, they may provide practical advice, suggestions, or actionable steps that empower you to take control of your life and create positive change.

Toward the end of the session, your Guide may invite you to ask any additional questions or seek clarification on any topics covered during the reading. This is your opportunity to address any lingering doubts or uncertainties you may have.

Are psychic readings confidential?

Our Guides understand the sensitive nature of the information shared during psychic readings and are committed to maintaining the highest standards of confidentiality and professionalism. Whether you're communicating with your psychic via phone, video call, or chat, your conversations are protected from unauthorized access.

If you prefer to remain anonymous during your psychic reading, you have the option to use a pseudonym or nickname instead of your real name. Your psychic will respect your privacy and address you by the name you choose.

For further information on how we safeguard your privacy, see our Privacy Policy.

How should I prepare for my reading?

Before the reading, find a quiet and comfortable space where you won't be interrupted, and make sure you have a reliable internet connection or phone signal. Take some time to quiet your mind and set your intentions. Reflect on the areas of your life where you're seeking guidance and clarity, and consider jotting down any questions or concerns you'd like to address during the session.

Your session will begin with an introduction and a brief explanation of how the reading will proceed. Your Guide will create a supportive and nonjudgmental atmosphere to help you feel comfortable and relaxed.

You can ask your Guide about any area of your life where you're seeking guidance. Your Guide will tap into their intuitive abilities to provide insights, messages, and guidance that resonate with your energy and circumstances.

They may use divination tools such as Tarot cards, astrology charts, or intuitive impressions to enhance the clarity and depth of the reading. Your Guide may also provide practical advice, suggestions, or action steps to help you navigate through challenges and obstacles, seize opportunities, and manifest your goals and desires.

After the reading, take some time to reflect on the insights and guidance you received. If you have any follow-up questions or need further clarification, reach out to your Guide.

    • Time credit will be issued based on session logs, which we retain for up to one month or 10 sessions.

    If you encounter any dissatisfaction, don't hesitate to reach out — we're here to support you every step of the way.
