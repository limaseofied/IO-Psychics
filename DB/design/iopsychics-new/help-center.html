What is IO Psychics?

IO Psychics is a spiritual platform that connects you with experienced Spiritual Guides for psychic and spiritual readings. We also offer tools like horoscopes and Tarot to support your journey.

How do I join the IO Psychics community?

Create a free account on our website to join the IO Psychics community.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require a 30‑day notice to cancel. Your plan remains active through your current billing period plus 30 days from your cancellation request; after that, it terminates automatically.

Will I receive a refund if I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are non‑refundable. Service continues through the current billing cycle and the 30‑day grace period. No refunds or proration for unused time.

What happens to my data and access after I cancel?

After termination (following the 30‑day grace period), access to premium features and stored data is revoked.

Do you offer customer support?

Yes. IO Psychics provides 24/7 support for both Guides and Seekers.
